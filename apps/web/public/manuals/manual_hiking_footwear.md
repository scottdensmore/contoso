## Technical Specs

**Waterproofing**: Advanced breathable membrane  
**Laces**: Heavy-duty woven nylon  

## User Guide
1. Wear your new boots indoors first to break them in.
2. Remove dirt and mud after every hike using a soft brush.
3. Dry slowly at room temperature away from direct heat.

## Cautions
1. Avoid drying near radiators or open fires.
2. Ensure correct lacing to prevent foot fatigue.
3. Clean regularly to maintain breathability.
