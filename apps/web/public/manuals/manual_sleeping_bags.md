# Sleeping Bag User Guide

## Features
- **Optimal Warmth**: High-loft insulation for superior heat retention.
- **Contoured Hood**: Ergonomic shape to keep your head and neck warm.
- **Snag-Free Zipper**: Smooth, easy operation with insulated draft tube.
- **Water-Repellent Shell**: Durable outer fabric shields against moisture.

## Technical Specs
**Insulation**: Down or synthetic high-loft  
**Temperature Rating**: 0°F to 45°F (varies by model)  
**Shell Fabric**: Ripstop nylon with DWR finish  
**Lining**: Soft touch moisture-wicking polyester  

## User Guide
1. Unroll the bag and allow it to loft for 10-15 minutes before use.
2. Use with an insulated sleeping pad for maximum comfort.
3. Use the hood drawstrings to trap heat on colder nights.

## Cautions
1. Keep away from fire and extreme heat sources.
2. Do not store compressed for long periods; use a large mesh storage bag.
3. Ensure the bag is completely dry before packing to avoid mildew.

## FAQ
1) Is it machine washable?
   Yes, but only in front-loading machines with technical down or synthetic wash.
2) Can two bags be zipped together?
   Most of our rectangular and mummy models feature compatible zippers for coupling.
3) How do I choose the right temperature rating?
   Select a rating slightly lower than the coldest temperature you expect to encounter.
