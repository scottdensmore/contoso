# Backpack User Guide

## Features
- **Ergonomic Support**: Padded shoulder straps and breathable back panel.
- **Ample Storage**: Multiple compartments and easy-access pockets.
- **Hydration Ready**: Internal sleeve for your hydration bladder.
- **Durable Build**: High-density nylon fabric.

## Technical Specs
**Volume**: 20L to 85L (varies by model)  
**Material**: 420D ripstop nylon  
**Zippers**: YKK water-resistant zippers  
**Weight**: Optimized for comfort  

## User Guide
1. Loosen all straps before putting the pack on.
2. Position the hip belt so it sits on your hip bones.
3. Tighten the shoulder straps to bring the pack close.

## Cautions
1. Do not overload beyond the recommended weight capacity.
2. Keep away from sharp objects to prevent tears.
3. Hand wash only with mild soap.
