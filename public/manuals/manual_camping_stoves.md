# Camping Stove Safety & Usage Guide

## Safety First
- **Ventilation:** Only use in well-ventilated outdoor areas. Never use inside a tent or vehicle.
- **Stability:** Place the stove on a flat, stable, non-flammable surface.
- **Clearance:** Keep flammable materials away from the burner.

## Operation
1. Connect the fuel canister securely.
2. Open the valve slightly to hear the gas hiss.
3. Ignite using the piezo igniter or a match.
4. Adjust the flame as needed.

## Maintenance
- Check O-rings and seals regularly for wear.
- Clean the burner head if it becomes clogged.
