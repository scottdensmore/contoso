## User Guide
1. Always follow the specific care label on your garment.
2. Use a technical detergent designed for outdoor gear.
3. Air dry whenever possible to extend garment life.

2. Do not use fabric softeners as they can impair technical performance.
3. Do not iron technical prints or logos.
