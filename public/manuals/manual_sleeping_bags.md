## FAQ
**Is it machine washable?**
Yes, use a front-loading machine and technical cleaner. Air dry or low heat.
**Can two bags be zipped together?**
Most of our rectangular and mummy bags feature compatible zippers.

## User Guide
- **Setup:** Unroll and allow to loft for 10 minutes before use.
- **Storage:** Store loose in a mesh bag, not compressed.

## Caution
- Keep away from fire and extreme heat.
- Ensure the bag is dry before long-term storage.
