## User Guide
1. Choose a flat, clear area to pitch your tent.
2. Unpack the tent and lay it flat.
3. Assemble the poles and insert them into the corresponding sleeves.
4. Raise the tent and secure the corners.

## Caution
- Keep all flame and heat sources away from this tent fabric.
- Do not use gas heaters inside the tent.
