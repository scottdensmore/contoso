# Tent User Guide

## Setup Instructions
1. Choose a flat, clear area to pitch your tent.
2. Unpack the tent and lay it flat.
3. Assemble the poles and insert them into the corresponding sleeves or clips.
4. Raise the tent and secure the corners with stakes.
5. Attach the rainfly if weather conditions require it.

## Care and Maintenance
- **Cleaning:** Clean with a sponge and mild soap. Do not machine wash.
- **Storage:** Ensure the tent is completely dry before packing to prevent mold. Store in a cool, dry place.
- **Repair:** Use the included patch kit for minor tears or holes.

## Safety
- Keep flames and heat sources away from the tent fabric.
- Ensure proper ventilation to prevent condensation and suffocation risks.
