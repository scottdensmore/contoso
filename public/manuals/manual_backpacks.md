## FAQ
**Is it waterproof?**
It is highly water-resistant; we recommend a rain cover for heavy downpours.
**Can it fit a 15-inch laptop?**
Most of our daypacks include a padded sleeve that fits up to a 15-inch laptop.

## User Guide
1. Loosen all straps before putting the pack on.
2. Position the hip belt so it sits on your hip bones.
3. Tighten the shoulder straps to bring the pack close.

## Caution
- Do not overload beyond the recommended weight capacity.
- Keep away from sharp objects.
